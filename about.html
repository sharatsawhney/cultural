ABOUT

Cultural Council or Cultural Societies, popularly referred to as ‘Cul-Soc’ is a student body which is responsible for all the cultural events in the institute round the year. Culture brings us all together and from togetherness comes success and this feature of life here certainly adds spice to the lives of the students.
