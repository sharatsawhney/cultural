Choreography and Dance Section, IIT Roorkee called as CHOREO in campus is one of the most popular performing section of the Cultural Council. With a core team of around 60 members, the section is responsible for performing dances in events held inside and outside the campus as well as choreographing them. CHOREO also participates in competitions such as Footloose held in Thomso, Inter IIT Cultural meet and fests of other colleges representing IIT Roorkee. The members also seek active participation from non-section members and involve them in Cultural Council by conducting events like Flashmob and Friends of Section. CHOREO is a team of dedicated and talented dancers who look forward to make the Institute proud along with cherishing their love for dancing.

Menka’s Cradle (Footloose, Thomso)

Footloose is the much awaited dance competition conducted in the Annual cultural fest of IIT Roorkee, Thomso with an active participation of over 50 colleges and participations over 250. CHOREO with its theme, “Devil’s Novella” mesmerized the judges as well as the audience with its Shadow dancing and bagged the first prize in the Group Dance category, menka’s Cradle.
