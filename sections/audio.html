Have you ever wondered how it would feel to sit in a closed room with absolutely no sound or music to hear, to feel… you would go completely mad, haven’t you? Well, to save the IITians from this madness, the Cultural Council of IIT Roorkee has setup the AUDIO SECTION. It is the section which manages all types of audio-related activities in the IITR campus, from the soft and soothing tunes of the Classical Night, to the hard-hitting, head banging songs of the Rock Show, Audio takes care of it all.
Audio Section, with an efficient team from all the years, provides the acoustics for events all over the year like Pehla Nasha, Swaranjali, Rock Show, etc. Loaded with state-of-the-art Aural and Mega Speakers, the main attraction of the Audio Section are its most awaited DJ nights, which are recognized as the best DJ nights of the campus, even better than the ones organized during Thomso.
But along with the core work of the section, Audio section has been regularly evolving and the latest feather in its already illustrious cap is its very own Online radio - CampusBeats @ 192.168.124.246 (www.mixlr.com/campusbeats) which is a first of its kind in all IITs, a step forward in the endeavor of bringing the campus, its very own ON-AIR radio. CampusBeats provides the students with an Online Radio where they can listen to scheduled programs and all sort of music. It provides infotainment to the campus for which it has featured programmes like TOP20 songs of the week, Club Beats, Campus Tadka, Just 4 U, Celeb Chat, Rock Opera and a lot more.
