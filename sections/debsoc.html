The Debating Society, IITR is the official student body of IITR for English Parliamentary debaters on campus. It offers a platform for free discussion of all ideologies, beliefs and partisan subscriptions without itself subscribing to any particular view.The purpose of this organization is to acquaint its members with Asian Parliamentary Debating, which is an extemporaneous form of competitive debate stressing on rigorous argumentation, logical analysis, quick thinking, and breadth of knowledge. The Debating Society envisions to build a corpus of stellar debaters in IIT Roorkee, who can competently represent the institute at all Parliamentary Debating Tournaments in India and across the world.
