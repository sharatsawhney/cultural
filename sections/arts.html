Annual Event : Srishti

Srishti is the Annual Exhibition organised by the Fine Arts Section, IIT Roorkee. Rated very high in terms of creativity, it encompasses all the ideas a human mind can give wings to. Spanning over a couple of days, it showcases a collection of masterpieces created by the members of the section over the entire year. Be it a large scale model requiring loads of helping hands and months of continuous hard work or a hyper realistic sketch, requiring tons of patience and precision, one can witness such jaw dropping artworks in this annual exhibitions. Artworks from students outside the section are welcomed too and are displayed during the exhibition.

With the growth and increasing sophistication of humans, the need to express the original, experimental and innovative ideas making waves in their brain became urgent. This urge led to the birth of art and the sense of aesthetic in human life. Art is a construct and creation by human agency. Artistic images reflect & embody the finest of human feelings, their dreams & ideals. We simply can’t think of important moments in life without associated artistic tokens; from greeting cards to monuments, everything is art.

Art can be considered as an emergent property of a sufficiently broad intelligence. A way of expression of sapient beings, art is universal, and like language, it helps us communicate. Creativity is associated with higher brain function and thus, as students of a prestigious institute of higher learning, the onus is upon us to promote creativity in areas of visual/intellectual stimuli in the campus through the Fine Arts Section.

Fine Arts Section is a place where anyone can come and participate in this culture of creativity and aesthetic and its proliferation in the campus and beyond. A melting pot of creativity and imagination, the section has been a fountainhead of artistic brilliance and splendour throughout its long and illustrious history. The walls of the section that lay shrouded with a remarkable aura of grace and beauty, with contributions coming in right from the tiny tots of kindergarten to the well hailed veterans of the art, bear testimony to this fact.

“Every child is an artist. The problem is how to remain an artist once he grows up.”-Pablo Picasso. So here at Fine Arts Section, we let free our imagination and explore and satiate the artistic streak within everyone and everything.
