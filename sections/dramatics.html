Perhaps the oldest of the cultural sections of IIT Roorkee, Dramatics Section is a means to fulfill the passion for performing on stage, as well as in street plays. We are a team of insanely hard working people, with amazing camaraderie, who perform various plays round the year with arguably professional competency. We perform one stage play and one street play in autumn semester and 2 long length stage plays in spring semester. We also perform during Orientation Ceremony every year. We conduct Friend of Section in which people who are enthusiastic about acting and are not part of out section take part. Apart from that we take part in Inter-IIT Cultural Meet every year. From writing play, to direction and to performance, members get to work closely with each other throughout the year to up with the best of performances institute has seen.
