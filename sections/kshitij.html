Kavyanjali

Kavyanjali is the official annual poetic event of IIT Roorkee organized by Kshitij. It encompasses poetic performances by the students and is a platform for them to showcase their talent. It is a medium to reach out to the populace of the campus who have a knack for hindi literature.

Eunoia

Eunoia is the first ever Spoken Word Poetry event of IIT Roorkee. Combining poets from the English Editorial Section of Kshitij, other poets from the campus and some guest performers, the evening sees some very mesmerising performances being performed.

Kshitij is the official literary magazine of Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee- by the students , for the students . The basic aim of this bilingual magazine- with entries in English and Hindi- is to inculcate in the students of the institute a good reading habit and in turn spreading a literary culture in the campus.
