Music Section is a group of talented musicians who are enthusiastic young learners with a musical ear. Music section comprises of vocalists, percussionists, flautist, guitarist, pianist and others instrumentalists. We bring together the past and present through our musical performances. The music section aims to promote an artistic culture in the campus through its various events and the “Friends of Section” initiative. We believe music is a language everybody speaks. Music connects people, and it doesn’t have any boundaries. The members of Music Section, try and experiment various genres which is not bound by any language, caste. We try to create more music by innovating and experimenting among various genres and styles of play.

Also, one of our tasks includes performing the “Kulgeet”; the anthem of our premium institute, at the start of various important ceremonies hosted by our campus.

We, the members of the Music Section, IIT Roorkee seek to provide the community with quality music, and promote the same by means of shows, workshops, and the “Friends and Section” initiative. The Music Section was established under the aegis of DOSW, as one of the wings under the Cultural Council, IIT Roorkee. The members use the given platform to showcase their musical knowledge and skill, and we intend to explore and promote various genres of music such as pop, rock, jazz blues, classical, rap, fusion between Indian and western style of music, and many others.

As a wing of the cultural council, we will uphold the cultural integrity and values of the council and promise to respect the cultural diversity of campus. We shall strive to contribute to the campus and its people. We pledge to abide by this constitution, approved by the Dean of Student’s Welfare and the Cultural Council, IIT Roorkee, and the procedural guidelines and protocols mentioned herein shall be implemented under all circumstances.
